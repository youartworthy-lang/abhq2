📐 HOW EST $/MO IS CALCULATED — READ THIS
Score (0–100) = Age Demand ×40% + Edition Market ×40% + Format ×20%
Higher = more buyer demand for this combination.

Est $/mo per listing = Score-weighted visits × 3% conv × price × 93% (after Etsy fees)
Formula: (visits/listing × score^0.7 × 0.03 × price) × 0.93
⚠️ Why does Rank #1 show only $2/mo?
Est $/mo uses your current shop average: ~52 visits/listing/mo from real Etsy data. With 32 live listings and 1,649 visits, that's ~52 visits each. A score-100 listing at 52 visits × 3% conv × $12.95 = ~$20/mo. As you publish more listings and traffic grows, every listing earns more.
